Scoutbox is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, username, email, password, and profile picture.
  • User-Generated Content: Sports highlights, photos, videos, and spectator videos.
  • Device & Usage Data: Device type, app version, operating system, and crash logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Let you create, share, and view sports highlight clips.
  • Improve app performance, features, and security.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Service Providers: For video hosting, analytics, push notifications, and moderation tools.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, court order, or to enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Your Controls

  • Delete or edit your content at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at spoit@spoit.io or filling out this form.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee complete security of transmissions over the Internet.

6. Data Processing

We process data for the purposes of promoting amateur players and improving Scoutbox's future AI services.

7. Children's Privacy

Scoutbox is intended for general audiences and can be used by individuals aged 3 and older. However, under Korean law, users under the age of 14 must have parental or legal guardian consent before creating an account or submitting personal information, photos, or videos. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14 without such consent. Parents or guardians can request deletion of their child’s account or data at any time via spoit@spoit.io or by filling out this form.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The Last Updated date above will reflect the latest version.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, please contact spoit@spoit.io.
